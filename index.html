About us

REBECA as the word says, REunion and Bengal Engineering College Annuals, from it’s very inception in 1932, this has been the annual reunion of the then BE college. Later on it has been transformed into an annual college fest cum reunion, well known for its magnanimity in eastern India. This is the annual festival of fun and fiesta. On this 81st edition of REBECA, we believe the unity amongst the students and ever growing presence of our alumni will be more evident than ever. So let’s all reunite and ignite the colorful and evergreen spirits of BEings.
